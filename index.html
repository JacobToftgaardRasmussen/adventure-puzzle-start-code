How to play

The user controls the player square using the arrow keys.

  • This is the player.
  • These squares are boxes.
  • These squares are rocks.
  • These squares are a cactus
  • These squares are skeletons
  • These squares are called sand.
  • These squares are walls.
  • White squares are air (empty).

If a box or rock is not supported by anything, it falls. The player can push one rock or box at a time, provided it is not obstructed or falling. The path between the box and the lower-right corner is initially obstructed by a skeleton, so the player has to get a cactus to remove it. Sand can be "eaten" (removed) by the player by stepping on it.
